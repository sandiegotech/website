Shade

A privacy posture advisor for iPhone. Shade answers one question: how exposed is this phone right now? Bluetooth, location, network, app permissions — all visible at a glance, with guided steps to reduce what you're broadcasting.

View on GitHub ← All Tools
What it does

Know your exposure. Reduce it.

Most people have no idea how much their iPhone broadcasts.

Shade checks your phone's privacy posture across the signals that matter: Bluetooth state, network status, location permissions, and the apps that have access to more than they need. The exposure score gives you a clear read on where you stand. Mission modes — Everyday, Travel, Meeting, and Ghost — give you a structured way to tighten your posture for the situation you're in.

The manual checklist covers iOS settings Apple doesn't expose to ordinary apps, walking you through the exact path to review each one. A local app risk guide explains what the apps already on your phone can access. Everything stays on-device — no cloud, no account, no irony.

Platforms iOS — built with SwiftUI. Open GhostMode.xcodeproj in Xcode and run the target on a device or simulator.
