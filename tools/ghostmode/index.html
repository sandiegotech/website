What it does

Know your exposure. Reduce it.

Most people have no idea how much their devices broadcast.

Ghost Mode answers one question: how exposed is this device right now? Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, cellular, NFC, active listeners, outbound traffic destinations — all of it made visible. Mission modes (Everyday, Travel, Meeting, Blackout) provide a structured way to tighten your posture for the situation you're in. The posture checklist shows what is secure, what needs review, and exactly what action to take.

Every version is intentionally dependency-light and easy to audit. Local-only persistence — nothing leaves the device.

Available on three platforms

Each version is a separate native app, built for its platform's security model.

macOS

Ghost Mode — Mac

Persistent menu bar indicator, compact popover for switching exposure levels, live traffic visibility using local socket data, security scan, and an admin helper for real network lockdown actions.
