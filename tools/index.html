The ToolsFive Apps

Simple instruments, plainly made.

Each tool does one thing well, costs nothing, and keeps nothing of yours. The source for every one is public.

Focus

One button, a calm countdown, a daily goal that compounds into deep work. Discipline over motivation. iOS & macOS.
About → Source →

Gloss

Select a word — or a whole paragraph — anywhere on your iPhone and understand it in the context you found it. iOS.

Scope

A quiet menu-bar window into what your Mac is actually doing — power, memory, storage, and network at a glance. macOS.
About → Source →

Shade

See what your phone gives away, harden it, keep sensitive files in an encrypted vault, and — for members — route traffic through a no-logs VPN. On-device, no account. iOS.

The Gallery

Your television becomes an art gallery: one spotlit work at a time, music from the moment it was made, and the full story on a placard. Apple TV.
