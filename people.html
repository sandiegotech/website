A few good people, early.

The work is real and the seats are few — we would rather grow slowly with the right people than quickly with the wrong ones. The founding team is forming now, and we will introduce them by name as we launch, which is soon. Here is who we're looking for:

Students

People who want to come and study in person — in the workshop, the seminar, and the water. The first is confirmed; a few more places remain. As a Student →

Fellows

Scholars and builders who want to help design and build the Institute itself — a small founding group. As a Fellow →

Advisors

Practitioners and scholars willing to guide the founding — including experience with accreditation and institution-building. Write to us →

Patrons

Friends and benefactors who want to back the work and help keep it free. As a Patron →
