Attention & Deep Work

Focus

The world is engineered to pull your attention away from what matters. Focus is an app to help you get it back — structured sessions, gentle accountability, and the clarity to do deep work in an age of constant distraction.

  • Structured work and rest sessions built around real cognitive limits
  • Distraction blocking and gentle mode for when you need quiet
  • Daily intention setting — one clear priority before the phone takes over
  • No accounts, no feed, no algorithm

Privacy & Digital Awareness

Shade

Companies and apps track far more of your life than most people realize. Shade helps you understand what is being collected about you, block what you can, and make informed decisions about your digital footprint — without needing a computer science degree.

  • Plain-language breakdown of what apps and trackers collect
  • Network-level ad and tracker blocking
  • Privacy audit for the apps already on your phone
  • No irony — Shade itself collects nothing about you

Performance & System Health

Disko

Your Mac accumulates digital clutter, runaway processes, and performance drags that compound silently over time. Disko shows you exactly what is happening inside your machine and what to do about it — clear, actionable, and honest about what it finds.

  • Live performance overview — CPU, memory, disk, and heat at a glance
  • Storage audit identifying large files, caches, and safe-to-delete items
  • Startup and background process management
  • Plain explanations for every recommendation — no dark patterns

Open Source

MIT Licensed — use it for anything.

Every SDIT tool is published under the MIT License. Read the source, build on it, distribute it, or integrate it into your own projects. The only thing you cannot do is re-sell the apps as your own or remove attribution. Software built for the public good should be inspectable by the public.
