Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes. SDIT is a 501(c)(3) public charity (EIN 39-2954006). Donations are deductible to the extent permitted by U.S. law. You will receive an IRS-compliant receipt via email automatically after your gift is processed.
Can I set up a recurring gift?
Yes — and it is the most impactful way to give. Recurring gifts let us plan ahead and sustain programs over time. You can set a monthly or annual amount directly in the donation form above.
How is the money used?
Gifts support open curriculum development (45%), fellowships for those who need assistance (30%), institutional operations (15%), and future campus & facilities (10%). Questions? Write to us.
Other ways to give — stock, wire, employer match?
We welcome stock donations, wire transfers, and employer-matched gifts. Write to brandon@sandiegotech.org and we will work with you directly.
Where is SDIT right now?
SDIT is in its founding phase — building the framework, assembling the founding circle, and beginning the first programs. We work from shared spaces and borrowed rooms. The goal is a permanent campus. Your support funds each step of that journey.
