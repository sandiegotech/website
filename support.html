Why It MattersThe case

Wisdom has no price tag. Keeping it that way does.

Most institutions are built for those who can already afford them. SDIT is built for everyone else — for everyone who believes that the ability to think clearly, question deeply, and act with integrity should not depend on where you were born or what you can pay.

Real education still requires real resources — people, platforms, tools, and eventually a physical home. Curriculum writers, research fellows, and the infrastructure beneath them all have costs. Every gift funds that work directly and moves the institution forward.

Where We AreTransparency

Humble beginnings. Serious intentions.

SDIT is in its earliest days. We work from shared spaces, borrowed rooms, and wherever a committed group of people can gather and build. We are not pretending otherwise — this is what starting something real looks like.

The goal is a permanent, dedicated campus — a physical home for the kind of education we are building. Getting there requires sustaining the work that comes first: the curriculum, the fellows, the research, the infrastructure. Your support funds each step of that journey.

Before You GiveCommon questions

Common questions.

Deductible?

Yes. SDIT is a registered 501(c)(3) public charity (EIN 39-2954006). Donations are deductible to the extent permitted by U.S. law, and you will receive a receipt via email.

Used how?

Gifts support three areas: open curriculum development, fellowships for those who need assistance, and institutional operations. We publish a breakdown in our annual disclosure.

Recurring?

Yes — and it is the most impactful way to give. It lets us plan ahead and sustain programs long-term. You can set up a recurring gift through the PayPal button above.

Other ways?

We welcome stock donations, employer matching, and other forms of support. Write to brandon@sandiegotech.org and we will work with you directly.
