Every item here is checked against the tenets before it ships — above all the Artifact: knowledge is completed in the making. A plan that makes nothing is decoration.

IIThe Daily · Email

The habit, automated.

The Daily page is live. The one real gap is delivery: there is no email backend yet. The intent is a simple, privacy-respecting list — manual now, automated soon.

Building

Interim — manual list

Today the subscribe button opens an email to brandon@sandiegotech.org. Names are added to the list by hand. This works for the founding phase and costs nothing.

Planned

Automated — a real subscribe form

Recommended: Buttondown. It is built for exactly this — a plain daily letter, privacy-respecting, no surveillance, a free tier to start, and an embeddable HTML form that needs no JavaScript. It fits the aesthetic and the values.

Alternatives if needed: MailerLite (larger free tier), Kit/ConvertKit (creator-focused), or self-hosted Listmonk (free, but needs a server).

Handoff: the account requires a signup, so the founder creates it; hand over the username (or embed snippet) and the subscribe form on the Daily page can be wired to it in a couple of minutes, replacing the interim mailto. Sending the letter itself stays manual until a cadence is set.

Doctrine

On participation and fellowship

The Founder · MMXXVI Anyone is welcome to visit and stay and contribute. Your acceptance is earned not decided. Show up, put in the work. Fellowships are reserved for those who are driven the most. Not by who might make the greatest things, but by who will inspire others.

The floor is zero — no credential, no application required to follow the work, engage with the ideas, or contribute. The fellowship bar is different: not résumé, not potential output — drive, presence, and the capacity to pull others forward. The question is not what will you make but who will you move.
